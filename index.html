PM Portfolio Project #3 · Sun Cerrae Quinones
OnboardOS — Onboarding Process Portal
A solution to a problem I solved across three dental locations — where every office had a different onboarding process, leading to inconsistent training, compliance gaps, and new hire confusion.
Problem
Inconsistent onboarding led to claim rejections and compliance errors across 3 sites
Solution
Centralized process portal with checklist, flowchart, RACI, and document library
Outcome
25% claim rejection drop + measurably faster new hire ramp time at Charlotte Dentistry
Context: At Charlotte Dentistry, I developed standardized workflows and documentation that reduced errors and improved onboarding efficiency. At IllustraDENT, I managed multi-site operations and process improvements that enhanced data accuracy. This portal codifies that methodology.

Stack: Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS · Responsive sidebar layout · Interactive checklist with localStorage persistence · SVG-free swimlane diagram

Methodology: Process documentation · Stakeholder matrix (RACI) · 30/60/90 framework · Cross-functional coordination
