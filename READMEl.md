## ✨ Features

### 🏠 Overview
- Visual summary of all portal sections with clickable navigation cards
- At-a-glance stats: 25% rejection drop, 3 sites standardized, 90-day ramp timeline

### ✅ 30/60/90 Day Checklist
- Interactive milestone tracker across three onboarding phases
- Click any item to mark complete — progress persists via localStorage
- Overall progress bar with live percentage display
- One-click reset for reuse with each new hire

### 🔀 Process Flowchart (Swimlane Diagram)
- 4-lane table: HR · Manager · IT · New Hire
- 4 phases: Pre-Start · Day 1–7 · Days 8–30 · Days 31–90
- Color-coded by role with descriptive step cards

### 👥 RACI Matrix
- 8 onboarding tasks mapped across 4 roles
- R (Responsible) · A (Accountable) · C (Consulted) · I (Informed)
- Notes column explaining ownership logic for each task

### 📄 Document Template Library
- 6 standardized templates: offer checklist, 90-day goals, IT access form, 30-day check-in script, training log, performance review

## 📐 Frameworks Used

### 30/60/90 Day Model
A structured onboarding framework that breaks the first 90 days into three distinct phases, each with clear milestones and success criteria. Reduces new hire anxiety and gives managers a repeatable process.

### RACI Matrix
A responsibility assignment matrix that eliminates ambiguity on cross-functional tasks. Every onboarding activity has exactly one Accountable owner — preventing the "I thought you were handling that" failure mode.

### Swimlane Flowchart
A process diagram organized by role (lane) and time (column), making it immediately clear who needs to do what at each phase — without a meeting to explain it.
